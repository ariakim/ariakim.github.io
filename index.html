About

I'm a software developer and writer who believes that technology should be human-centric as all technology was made by humans, for humans. Whether this comes in the form of websites, brain-computer interfaces, or video games, I always keep this in mind. Because of this, I also value holding a degree of well-roundedness, which helps keep me perspective and open-minded in any task.
